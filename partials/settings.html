The Alchemist's Cookbook

Impure Threshold
"Impure" potions and poisons are created from combinations of ingredients that have matching beneficial and harmful ingredients.
An impure potion must have at least this many beneficial effects (harmful effects for poisons) to create a recipe for it.
The default is 3, to keep the recipe lists from being inflated with garbage that doesn't help the player.
Exclude Jarrin Root
If checked, no recipes will be generated from Jarrin Root, due to there only being one sample in the vanilla game.
Exclude Saints and Seducers Ingredients
New ingredients for Saints and Seducers are added to Update.esm. If checked, the patcher will not generate recipes for these ingredients, since they are not normally obtainable without the creation.
Verbose Logging
The patcher will log what it is doing. Useful for interpreting the progress bar.
